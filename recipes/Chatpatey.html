Chatpatey

Description

Chatpate is a popular snack in every street stalls in Nepal and India. It contains mouthwatering flavors and there are various kinds of recipes for chatpate.

Today we will learn how to make regular Nepali chatpatey.

Ingredients:

  • Puffed rice
  • Finely chopped onion
  • Tomato
  • Green chili peppers
  • Green peas
  • Cilantro
  • Salt
  • Wai wai noodles
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Chili powder
  • Finely chopped cucumber
  • Lemon juice
  • Mustard oil

Steps:

  1. In a big bowl, mix peas, chopped onion, tomato, potatoes, chilies, and cucumber together
  2. Add dalmot, wai wai noodles, and puffed rice.
  3. Add cumin powder, chili powder, wai-wai noodle masala, a pinch of salt or black salt.
  4. Add a tablespoon of mustard oil and then mix it properly.
  5. Squeeze some lemon juice and give it a stir.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander on top.
  7. It is now ready to serve.
