Samosa

Description

Samosas are fried, flaky dough wrapped around fillings of all kinds. They are popular South Asian snacks, commonly filled with potatoes and spices. They can be served as appetizers, entrees, or a lunch on the move.

Today in this recipe, we will be learning how to make samosas.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Green chili peppers
  • Green peas
  • Cilantro
  • Salt
  • Ginger
  • Ajwain seeds
  • Garam masala

Steps:

  1. Boil potatoes until tender, then use a fork to roughly mash.
  2. Fry up spices with fresh ginger and chili.
  3. Break off 1/2 oz of dough, form it into a ball, and roll in into a 4-inch round
  4. Add the mashed potato and gently but thoroughly mix through.
  5. Lastly, mix through fresh coriander, then let filling cool before using.
  6. Wrap the filling in a half-round of the wrapper.
  7. Fold one side in and form a cone.
  8. Seal the end and fry the samosas.
  9. Voila, you're done!
