Momos

Description

Nepali momos are dumplings filled with meat and/or vegetables and spices, and served with a spicy tomato based sauce. This dish is one of the favorite dish that will win over any Nepalese person's heart.

Today in this recipe, we will be learning how to make chicken momos.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Onion
  • Green chili peppers
  • Cabbage
  • Cumin powder
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Mix flour and water in a bowl in the form of dough
  2. Mix the chicken, vegetables, spices, and soy sauce
  3. Break off 1/2 oz of dough, form it into a ball, and roll in into a 4-inch round
  4. Place a tablespoon of the filling in thr wrapper
  5. Holding the wrapper in your hand, use your right and index finger to pinch the edges together. Pinch and fold until you can close the wrapper like a satchel.
  6. Steam the momos for 25 minutes.
